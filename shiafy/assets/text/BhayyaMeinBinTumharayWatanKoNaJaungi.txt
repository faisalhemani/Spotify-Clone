<p>JAB BAAD-E- DAFN-E- SHAAH WATAN KO HARAM CHALE
SAJJAD SAATH BEWOUN KE BAA CHASHME NAM CHALE
HAMRAAH LEKE QAAFELA-E- RANJ-O- GHAM CHALE
ZAINAB PUKAARI PEET KE SAR BHAAI HAM CHALE</p>

<p>ULFATH JO THI KAMAALE SHAH-E- MASHRAQAIN SE
ROI LIPAT LIPAT KE MAZAAR-E- HUSSAIN SE</p>

<p>PHIR SAR KO RAKH KE QABR PA WO GHAM KI MUBTELAA
BOLI KE ASSALAAM ALAIKAA AYE SHAH-E- HUDAA
BHAYYA UTHO SAWAAR KARO TUM PA AMAI FIDAA
FARMAAIYE WATAN ME MAI JAAKAR KAROONGI KYAA</p>

<p>RAH RAH KE DHYAAN AATHA HAI MUJH DIL MALOOL KO
KYA JAAKE MU DIKHAAUNGI QABRE RASOOL KO</p>

<p>BHAYYA MEIN BIN TUMHAAREY WATAN KO NA JAAOONGI
BHAYYA YAHIN MY QABR PAR AANSOO BAHAAOONGI
NAALOUN SEY AASMAANO ZAMEEN KO HILAAOONGI
NAZREY HUSSAIN PYAASOUN KO PAANI PILAAOONGI</p>

<p>JO POOCHEGAA TU ROTI HAI KYON SHOR-O SHAIN SEY
KEHDOONGI CHUTT GAYI HOON MY BHAAI HUSSAIN SEY</p>

<p>SAJJAD ROKAY KARTHAY THEY ZAINAB SEY YEH SUQAN
CHALIYEH PHUPI KHUDA KYA LIYE AB SU-EY WATAN
RO RO KAY JAAN DOH NA YAHAAN BEHRAY ZAULMEINAN
HONA HAI QAID TUMKO DOBAARA BASADH MEHAAN</p>

<p>RAUZEY PAH NAANA JAAN KAY AB JAAKAY ROYE-Y E
DURROU KAY ZAQM PUSHTH PA DIKHLAAKAY ROYE-YE</p>

<p>YEH SUNKAY UTTHI QABR SEY AAKHIR WOH SOGHVAAR
EK HAUDAJEH SIYAAH MEY AAKAR HUVI SAWAAR
PAHOONCHEY WATAN MEY JIS GHADI SAJJAD -E DILFIGAAR
PEHLAY GAYI BAQIYEH MEY ZEHRA KI GUL-E ZAAR</p>

<p>BOLI LIPAT KAY QABR SEY AMMA DUHAAYI HAI
BHAAI KO KHOKAY ZAINAB-E NAASHAAD AAYI HAI</p>

<p>AMMA SUNAOON TUMKO MAI JOH KUCH JAFAA HUVI
PARDES MEY HUSSAIN SEY ZAINAB JUDAA HUVI
CHOWTHEY BARAS MEY BAAP PAH BETI FIDAA HUVI
AMMA HUSSAIN MARGAYE MY BE-RIDAA HUVI</p>

<p>DIL ZINDAGI SEY ZAINAB-E MUZTAR KA HATT GAYA
MY DEKHTI RAHI SAR-E SHABBIR KATT GAYA</p>

<p>AMMA TUMHAAREY LAAL KO KYA KYA NA GHAM HUVEY
LAASHEY KAY HAATH ANGOTI KI QAATIR QALAM HUVEY
LAASHEY PAH MEREY BHAAYI KAY TAAZA SITAM HUVEY
PAAMAAL LAASH HOGAYI BARBAAD HUM HUVEY</p>

<p>GOWHAR SAKINA KAY BHI UTHAAREY HAIN SHIMR NEY
DURREY HAMAARI PUSHTH PA MAAREY HAIN SHIMR NEY</p>

<p>AMMA TERAY MAREEZ KO QAIDI BANAAYA THA
TOUQ-E GIRAAN BUKHAAR MEY USKO PINHAAYA THA
KAATOUN PA NANGEY PAAON ADDO NEY PHIRAAYA THA
AMMA SIVAA-E SHUKR NAHIN LABB HILAYA THA</p>

<p>BULWAAYA THA YAZEED NEY DARBAAR-E AAM MEY
AMMA SAKINA MARGAYI ZINDAAN-E SHAAM MEY</p>

<p>AYE AMMA EK RASSI MEY BANDHWAAYA THA HAMEY
OONTOUN KI NANGI PUSHTH PA BITTLAAYA THA HAMEY
SAR TASHTH MEY HUSSAIN KA DIKHLAAYA THA HAMEY
ABID KO DURREY MAAR KAY RULWAAYA THA HAMEY</p>

<p>HAIRATH HAI KYON NA ZAINAB-E NAASHAAD MARGAYI
AMMA MY SAR KHULEY HUVE SHIREEN KAY GHAR GAYI</p>

PAHOONCHEY JO SHAHER-E SHAAM MEY HUM SAB KUSHAADA MU
BAAZARIYON MEY SHOR YEH HOTHA THA CHAAR SU</p>
